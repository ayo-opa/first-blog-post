I didn't give order to suspend Big Brother Naija - Lai Mohammed

The minister of information and culture, Lai Mohammed, has debunked claims that he directed the Nigerian Broadcasting Commission (NBC) to suspend the Big Brother Naija show. A statement made by Mohammed's media aide, Segun Adeyemi, outrightly distanced the minister from the purported order to the NBC, PM News reports. Adeyemi said that if the minister gave the order to the commission concerning the reality show, it would have come in the form of a memo and not just verbally.
